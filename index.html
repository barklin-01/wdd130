Ecuador

Otavalo is a town in the Imbabura province of northern Ecuador, famous for its artisan market, one of the largest in South America. It is known for textiles, handmade crafts, and traditional jewelry, as well as the rich culture of the Otavalo indigenous community. Surrounded by Andean landscapes like lakes, volcanoes, and mountains, it is a popular tourist destination.
